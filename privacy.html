1. Introduction

SaaS Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our content management platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you use SaaS Pro with TikTok integration, we may collect:

2.1 Information from TikTok OAuth

  • Open ID: A unique identifier for your TikTok account
  • Display Name: Your TikTok username
  • Profile Picture: Your TikTok avatar URL
  • Access Token: To publish content on your behalf

2.2 Content You Upload

  • Video files you choose to publish
  • Titles and descriptions you provide
  • Campaign metadata and settings
Important: We only request the minimum TikTok permissions necessary to provide our service (user.info.basic and video.upload).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session
  • Display your profile information in our dashboard
  • Publish videos to TikTok on your behalf when you request
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • TikTok: To publish content as authorized by you
  • Service Providers: Who assist in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure OAuth token storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your TikTok account at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with TikTok. When you connect your TikTok account, your use of TikTok is also governed by:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Terms of Service

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
